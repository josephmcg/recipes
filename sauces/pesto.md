# Pesto

## Ingredients

- 40 +/- 5 basil leaves
- 28g pine nuts
- 2 cloves garlic
- .5 tsp salt
- .25 tsp pepper
- .25 cup (20g) parmesan
- .25 cup (30g) olive oil

## Instructions

- Crush salt and garlic w/ mortar and pestle, form a paste
- Add nuts, crush
- Gradually add basil leaves. Crush, not grind
- Add cheese
- Slowly add oil
